| ALB 다중 AZ 구성        |  3 | aws elbv2 describe-load-balancers --names WorldPay-ALB --query "LoadBalancers[0].AvailabilityZones[].ZoneName" --output text                                                                                                                                                             | ap-northeast-2a ap-northeast-2c |                                                                                                                 |
